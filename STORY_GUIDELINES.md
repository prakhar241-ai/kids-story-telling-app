# Story Guidelines — KidsAreBest22

**Every story in this app (database seed, API prompts, or anywhere) MUST follow these rules.**

## STRICT CONTENT RULES — never violate these
- No violence, fighting, weapons, or injury of any kind
- No scary, horror, or nightmare-inducing content
- No death (of people or animals)
- No sexual content, romance, or adult themes
- No bad language, insults, or bullying behavior
- No dark emotions like deep sadness, trauma, or fear
- No villains that are threatening or terrifying
- No real-world danger like drugs, alcohol, or crime

## STORY STYLE RULES
- Always end with a happy, positive ending
- Include a simple moral lesson (friendship, kindness, sharing, bravery, honesty)
- Use simple words suitable for young readers
- Characters should be lovable (animals, kids, friendly creatures, magical beings)
- Keep sentences short and easy to read
- Add light humor where possible to keep kids engaged
- Story should feel warm, cozy, and imaginative
- Use vivid but child-friendly descriptions

## FORMAT
- Give the story a fun title
- Break into 3–4 short paragraphs (2–3 for toddlers, age 2–3)
- End with a one-line moral like: `The End! Remember: [lesson]`

## SAFETY FEATURES (app)
- **Keyword input filter:** if the entered keyword contains inappropriate words, show:
  `Oops! Try a fun word like 'unicorn' or 'spaceship'! 🌈`
- **Blocked keywords:** violence, weapon, death, kill, blood, scary, horror, sex, drug, alcohol, hate (and common variations)
- **Max keyword length:** 30 characters
- **No user data stored** anywhere

## ADDITIONAL FEATURES
- **"Surprise Me! 🎲"** button — picks a random keyword from a fun list (dinosaur, mermaid, robot, cupcake, jungle, wizard, unicorn, spaceship…)
- **Font size toggle:** Small / Medium / Large
- **Emoji reaction bar** below the story: 😍 🤩 😄 (just for fun, no data saved)
